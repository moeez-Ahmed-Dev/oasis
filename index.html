Frequently Asked Questions

What makes EcoFinish better than traditional pool plaster or paint? +
EcoFinish is a thermally applied polymer finish that creates a non-porous, smooth, and flexible membrane over your pool shell. Unlike traditional pool plaster which chips, discolors, and degrades in 5-7 years, EcoFinish is chemical resistant, pH neutral, stain-resistant, and backed by a 10-year manufacturer warranty.
How long does it take before we can fill the pool with water? +
Immediately! Unlike plaster finishes that require a 28-day curing cycle and strict acid washing routines, EcoFinish cures instantly as it is applied. As soon as application is finished, water filling can begin right away.
What is an ICF Pool, and why choose ICF construction? +
ICF stands for Insulated Concrete Forms. We assemble interlocking expanded polystyrene forms reinforced with steel rebars, then pour concrete inside. This creates perfectly straight, ultra-strong pool walls with built-in R-value insulation, keeping pool water warmer and lowering heating costs.
Can EcoFinish be applied to fiberglass pools? +
Yes! We use polyFIBRO®, specifically engineered for fiberglass pools. It bonds directly to gelcoat or repaired fiberglass, expanding and flexing with the pool structure to prevent cracking.
